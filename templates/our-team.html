Our Superiors

Messages from our Hardworking Superiors

Hashmatullah Helali

It is a pleasure to represent this outstanding organization, Aid in Action Economic and Social Assistance Services Organization (AAESSO). We have served the Afghan community for the past several years. We plan to continue providing quality resources to the Afghanistan People Aid in Action Economic and Social Assistance Services Organization, is truly thankful to those members of AAESSO, who had the vision since several years for this Foundation. Through the perseverance of those with the passion for this charitable endeavor, today, 2022, we can say the vision is still a reality. AAESSO is dedicated to enhancing the quality of life of the residents throughout Afghanistan. Through its yearly “Debutante, signature fundraiser, programs and other fundraising opportunities, AAESSO, is able to assist and partner in charitable, artistic, educational, economic development activities with various community organizations.

PRESIDENT of Organization

M.Haroon Noorzai

I'm pleased to introduce the Afghan non-profit organization "Aid in Action Economic and Social Services Organization (AAESSO)". I extend a warm greeting on behalf of AAESSO. With such aims and objectives, it was founded with a strong message for the many purposes, to contribute to the development of Afghanistan and the advancement of our nation in numerous sectors. AAESSO was founded with the goal of advancing underprivileged children, women, elderly people, charitable individuals, etc. in the fields of education, health, sustainable development, etc. Some of the fundamental rights that every person in the globe should have access to, irrespective of their social class, are the right to education and the right to health. The AAESSO does everything possible to give every person who has been denied these right the opportunity to exercise them. At the same time, we want to provide individuals the skills they need to access all of life's basics, so that they can gain respect and dignity for themselves and the generations to come. We are incredibly appreciative of the help we receive from numerous individuals who enable us to carry out our work. If you are a youth, feel free to visit us and participate in our events at any time. We always appreciate any kind of assistance that is given.

CEO of Organization

Nazifa Azimi

This organization was established to assist the underprivileged in obtaining a fundamental education and access to healthcare in order to live a healthy life in the future. We are appreciative of the generous assistance we receive from many people, and we appreciate them for their commitment to our cause. Our Society strives to make a bigger impact in our field and is committed to its work. Our work doesn't merely concentrate on people of a certain age. Along with the elderly, we offer assistance to children, young people, and women in need. We do this to help these people's growth and wellbeing so that they can both individually and collectively realize their full potential. I firmly think that in order to maintain everyone's wellbeing, we must empower families and communities to become self-sufficient. The next generation, whether wealthy or not, has the ability to improve this world. Our team puts a lot of effort into figuring out how to level the playing field for kids and teenagers with intellectual and developmental disabilities so that they may also contribute significantly to the growth of Afghanistan in the future. We constantly appreciate the assistance given by many sources that share our conviction about the need for change. I sincerely appreciate your assistance in sharing the word of hope.

CO-PRESIDENT of Organization
