About Us

Introduction:

Aid in Action Economic and Social Assistance Services Organization (AAESSO) considering the goals and policies of the adoption, It works in the field of women's empowerment and self-sufficiency, and it is needed in the implementation of projects Practical steps should be taken to empower women at the central and provincial levels. Our NGO has always tried to improve the living conditions of women in various fields and plans for useful projects in order to achieve its desired goals consolidate the stability of the rule of law and the growth of the economy and the empowerment of women throughout Afghanistan, so that many women in the country could use the opportunities to develop their skills and family's economy, And on one hand we witness the financial independence of women and on the other hand we witness reduction of poverty, family violence and active participation of women in economic growth in a family

Vision:

  • Supporting the work and activities of women in order to promote and develop the economy that will lead to their self-sufficiency;
  • Participation of women in work and job creation and encouragement to professional activities that cause the economic development of families;
  • Participation of women in the workforce, the creation of jobs, and encouragement of professions that contribute to the economic growth of families;
  • Exchange of experiences and transfer of professional teachings between women to the regional and community level;
  • Reduction in violence as a result of women's economic prosperity and their increased employment opportunities;
  • Raising the graph of women's inclusion in economic and professional education;
